Tutorial

First of all.

There is no gas fee, we won't send you any token or ask for your seed phrase!!!
The app is still in development so bugs can occur.
This app does not interact with your wallet, it just list your tokens for you.


Steps:

  • First go to the Dashboard.
  • On the Dashboard you have the choice to login using WalletConnect or Metamask.
  • Or you can just copy your wallet address and use that to list your tokens out.
  • After you see your tokens, you can load the current value of each.
    • It loads in how much you have from that specific token and multiplies it with the current value.
  • For better visibility you can hide unwanted tokens by pressing the "Hide" button.
  • If you miss clicked and hidden a token you didn't want to. You can unhide it.
    • Press the "List Hidden" button. It will list you hidden tokens.
    • By pressing the "Unhide" button, you can unhide them and they will show up.
  • You can unhide everything with one button, just press the "Clear Hidden" button and press "Yes".
